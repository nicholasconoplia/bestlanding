About

Hi, I'm the student who got tired of drowning in Canvas.

Before I built Canvo, I was stuck in the same spiral as every overwhelmed student: checking Canvas 10 times a day, screenshotting assignments, juggling sticky notes, Notion boards, and alarms — and still missing deadlines.

I thought there had to be something — something that just pulled everything I had to do into one clean to-do list, so I didn't have to hunt through modules and announcements and enter it all manually.

It's 2025. Everything already exists, right?

Nope. Not for me.

So I spent hours searching. And when I couldn't find it… I built it.

Canvo started during finals week — not because I had time, but because for once, I was finally ahead. I used my own early version to stay on track. It worked so well, I had enough breathing room to turn it into something real.

Now it's not just mine — it's yours.

Canvo is for every student who's ever felt like:

"I should be on top of things… but I'm always behind."

"I'm working hard — but still forgetting stuff."

"Everyone else looks organized, and I'm secretly drowning."

I built Canvo because I needed it. You get to use it because I refused to settle for feeling behind anymore.

This isn't some VC-backed startup. It's one student, building for others who get it. No logins. No tracking. Just a tool that thinks like we do — and actually helps.

You couldn't find this app before. But now, luckily for you — it exists.

Let's finally feel on top of things — for real this time.
